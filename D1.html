Industrial Designs

The first impression of an Industrial design is the flavors of a warehouse /factory or an urban loft.
Industrial interior design is characterized by a very bold and raw design appeal with exposed structural elements like bricks, hanging A/C ducts, iron/metal framework, wooden rafters/pillars, and concrete textures. However, Industrial design doesn’t restrict to bring in one or two comfortable recliners and sofas or even a dash of modern art pieces displayed tastefully in spaces to add “raw feel” or for a more “lived-in” feel.
